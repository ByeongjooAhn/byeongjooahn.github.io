Byeongjoo Ahn 안병주

I am a research scientist at Apple in Seattle. I received my PhD from Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Aswin C. Sankaranarayanan and Ioannis Gkioulekas. Prior to that, I earned my BS and MS degrees from Seoul National University, where I worked with Kyoung Mu Lee. Additionally, I worked as a research scientist at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology.

My first name is pronounced as “Be-Young-Joo.”
